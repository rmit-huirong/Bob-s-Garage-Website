WE'RE NOT AMAZON

Today, everybody wants what they want-- RIGHT NOW (including me). Most people think that if you place an order online or by phone, it automatically ships the same day, or maybe the next day. For mass-produced goods from abroad, that may work. It's impossible for us to work that way.

Some things can ship in a day or two, many in 7 - 10 days. But most of our work is custom work that has to be built by hand. Often a spare part will be hard to find or must be reproduced. And we have LOTS of builds on the books

The shipping department works every day Monday through Friday shipping domestic as well as international. Please know that we are working very hard to get your order to you. But we don't have any drones that deliver ... not yet anyway.

And thanks for being our customer. We always appreciate your business

Bob and the guys at Bob's Garage
